About This Tool

This platform is designed to help potato farmers in Kenya quickly identify crop diseases. You can either upload a potato leaf image or describe the symptoms you're seeing. Our AI will provide a diagnosis and recommend the best treatment or preventive measures.

Why Use It?

  • Fast and accurate disease detection using AI
  • Simple and easy-to-use interface
  • Practical recommendations tailored for Kenyan farmers
  • Access to guidance without needing an agricultural expert on site
